Overview

Nestled in the southern corridor of Pune, Dhankawadi boasts of many residential and commercial entities in and around the area. Being close to Taljai Hills which in itself is a landmark in the area and has been designated as a forest reserve, it enjoys all the privilege of having a picturesque locale while being a part of the cityscapes. The location is quite famous amongst the peers as it holds a community of like-minded individuals who have stayed nearby and it gives the perfect semblance of connection to disconnect to the audiences staying there.
​Built on a hill, so enjoys a height advantage over others﻿. ﻿﻿Serenely poised with uninterrupted views. ﻿﻿Vastu compliant homes because of the east-west facing layout. ﻿﻿No construction in and around the corner giving it a fruitful advantage. ﻿﻿Close to Taljai Hills - Cool breeze throughout the day.
