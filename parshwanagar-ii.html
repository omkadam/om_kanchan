Overview

What we've created is more than a residential project, it’s a layered experience. Paras Parshwanagar - II offers well-planned interiors for daily living and spaces that nurture reflection, connection, and aspiration. Here, homes go beyond walls and square footage, providing living spaces that understand and grow with you.

What makes a home complete? It’s the essential needs that shape our lives, choices, and well-being. Guided by Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs, our design philosophy addresses every level: physiological needs, safety, belonging, esteem, and self-actualization.

Paras Parshwanagar - II's elevation is modern, balanced, and purposeful. The architecture is understated, allowing community and calm to be the focus. Every detail is selected to stand the test of time, with a home that fits into your world with ease and meaning.

An entire floor is dedicated to thoughtful amenities—spaces for children to play, families to gather, and individuals to recharge. Each corner is designed for ease, connection, and balance. The rooftop amenity floor, with a 14-foot ceiling, is open and airy, seamlessly integrated into the project to support a complete and enriching lifestyle.
