Overview

For the one's who deserve the ‘One & Only Life’! The world is coming to PUNE'S NEW BKC at Mundhwa.
The 3 & 4 BHK upscale residences at Osian One & Only are planned so that neither the size of the homes nor your stature is compromised.

Perfectly Planned Layouts - The spacious layouts at Osian One & Only are thoughtfully planned to accommodate your every need, want & desire, quite literally.
